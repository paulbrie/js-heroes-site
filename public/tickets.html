JSHeroes 2017

JSHeroes is a not-for-profit community conference run by a team of volunteers from the Cluj JavaScripters community.

Early Bird Tickets are sold out.

As you might expect, each ticket includes:

  • 2 days fully packed with high-class JS talks,
  • premium catering lunch on both days (vegan options available) and coffee breaks

The meetup with the speakers, on June 7th, is not included in any of the conference tickets' price. For that, follow the updates and RSVP on the Cluj JavaScripters group from Meetup.com
